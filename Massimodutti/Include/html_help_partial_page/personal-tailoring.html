PERSONAL TAILORING

Can I customise my Personal Tailoring suit at the online shop?

Our online shop currently does not offer a Personal Tayloring suit and/or shirt customization service. If you want a customised Personal Tailoring suit or shirt, please visit any of our shops that offer this service. We will be happy to help you.

What our online shop can offer is a selection of suits and shirts, previously made with the strict manufacturing criteria of Personal Tailoring; these include the quality of the fabric or the unbeatable finishes of the garment.



Is the sizing system the same for all Personal Tailoring products?

The sizing system for suits is the same as the traditional system. We do not have half sizes

Blazers: European system: 48, 50, 52…

Trousers: European system: 38, 40, 42, 44

The sizing system for shirts is based on the traditional tailoring system: 38, 39, 40…



Is the quality used in Personal Tailoring different from the other suits and shirts?

Yes. The selection of "super" quality fabrics such as the "120/2 double yarn" of the cotton used in shirts and the so-called "super wool 130’s" in tailoring give Personal Tailoring garments premium quality, softness and comfort.

Are all Personal Tailoring trousers Slim Fit?

Yes. Currently, all Personal Tailoring trousers are Slim Fit, giving them a better, more stylish fit and increased quality.

Are shirts made in two possible fits?

No, each Personal Tailoring shirt is made based on a pattern and models. The product description shows whether it is a Slim Fit shirt, or a Tailored Fit shirt.

Do Personal Tailoring shirts come with spare collar stays?

Yes, each shirt comes with an extra set of collar stays to maintain their rigidity and height.

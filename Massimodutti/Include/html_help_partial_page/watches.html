Are Massimo Dutti watches water resistant?

You must pay attention to the degree of impermeability of each model. The impermeability is shown at the back of each watch (for example, "50M WATER RESISTANT"). In general, they are not watches expressly produced for practising water sports, so we recommend certain restrictions to their use. Water resistance is normally expressed in metres or atmospheres. 50M (metres) is equivalent to 5 ATM (atmospheres of pressure). This does not mean that the watch can be submerged down to 50 metres below the surface of the water, rather that it can be used in aquatic activities (swimming, etc.) without being submerged to a great depth. In any case, the wear to the leather strap may be greater the longer the watch is exposed to water.

There is international terminology to identify the water resistance of watches :
Water resistant - may resist splashing (hand washing, rain, etc.).
30 M (or 3 ATM) - may get wet (shower, etc.), but not be submerged in water for prolonged periods.
50 M (or 5 ATM) - may be used in aquatic activities (swimming, etc.) but not for snorkelling (immersion without oxygen tanks) or diving.

What guarantee period do Massimo Dutti watches have?

All our watches are guaranteed for a period of 2 years from the purchase date. This guarantee is limited, meaning that there are certain components not covered by it. For this purpose, we recommend that you carefully read the guarantee leaflet supplied with each watch.

What can I do if my watch is faulty?

A faulty watch can be taken to the store of your choice. The repair period will be a maximum of 30 days from reception of the faulty watch in the corresponding store. Please remember that for the guarantee to be valid, it is essential that the guarantee leaflet is presented, duly completed and stamped, together with the faulty watch (or where appropriate, a copy of the invoice or corresponding purchase slip).

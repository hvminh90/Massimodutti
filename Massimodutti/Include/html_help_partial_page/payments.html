PAYMENTS

What payment method can I use to make my purchase?

We accept the following payment methods: VISA, MasterCard, AMEX, Affinity, PayPal, GiftCard.

Is it safe to use my credit card on the website?

Yes, as the details you have sent us are processed in SSL encrypted form. For payment with Visa and Mastercard, only SEC (Safe Electronic Commerce) transactions will be accepted. Thus, after verifying that the card is associated with SEC, our systems will contact the card's issuing bank so that the purchase can be authorised. When the bank confirms the authenticity of the card, it will be charged. Otherwise, the order will be cancelled.

What should I do if my card is rejected?

Your card may be rejected for the following reasons:

- The card may have expired. Check that your card has not exceeded the validity period.

- The card may have reached its credit limit. Check with your bank that the card has not exceeded its credit limit.

- The details entered may be incorrect. Check that you have correctly completed the obligatory fields.

Can I receive an invoice in the name of my company?

Yes, you can receive an invoice in the name of your company by selecting the Company option when processing the order and providing the tax details we request.

Can I request Tax-Free?

No, you cannot obtain Tax-Free as this only applies to products bought in physical stores which are transported by the buyer themself.

FOOTWEAR

Are there special footwear collections for the online store?

No, we do not have special footwear collections for the online store.

Can I obtain a catalogue of the footwear models?

No, it is not possible to have a catalogue that shows which footwear models are available on the website at all times, since we introduce new footwear models in the online store every week. To this effect, we recommend that you visit our online store or contact our Customer Services Department, who will answer your questions about stock availability of a specific footwear model, by calling 900 456 000 or by sending an e-mail to contact@massimodutti.com

Will I receive the same footwear that I see in the photo?

Yes, you will receive the same footwear that you see in the photo, except in the case where a certain item of footwear has been produced following hand-crafted processes (brushing, waxing, greasing, ageing, etc.), which could produce minimal differences of finish.

What can I do if the footwear I receive is uncomfortable?

We only sell comfortable footwear so if, in an exceptional case, you receive an item of footwear that does not meet your needs, you can return or exchange it as long as it has not been used. For this purpose, please refer to our returns and exchanges policy, as well as our Customer Service Department.

Is Massimo Dutti suitable for any type of foot?

Yes, our footwear has been produced using standards which make it suitable for any type of foot.

Can Massimo Dutti footwear be used with inserts?

Yes, the footwear has been produced in such a way that it can be used with inserts. However if, in an exceptional case, you receive an item of footwear that does not meet your needs, you can return or exchange it as long as it has not been used. For this purpose, please refer to our returns and exchanges policy, as well as our Customer Service Department.

Can Massimo Dutti footwear be used by people with foot deformations?

Given that the footwear has been produced using standards, we cannot guarantee that it can be used by people with foot deformations.

Can Massimo Dutti footwear be harmful to people's health?

The aim of our product policy is not to involve risks to users' health. To this effect, we rigorously comply with our internal standard with regard to product health: Clear to Wear, which our suppliers are obliged to meet. In this way, we aim to guarantee that the products manufactured do not contain substances which, in certain concentrations, could be harmful to people's health. We check the fulfilment of this health standard using two monitoring processes: a prior one, in which the supplier is informed of the obligatory nature and implications of said standard, and in which they promise to exhaustively analyse their raw materials; and another subsequent one, through which we certify that the raw materials used in the manufacture of our products really meet this standard.

Can Massimo Dutti footwear involve risks to the safety of minors?

The aim of our product policy is that they do not involve risks to the safety of minors. To this effect, we rigorously comply with our internal standard with regard to product safety: Safe to Wear, which our suppliers are obliged to meet. In this way, we aim to protect minors by using relevant controls in both design and manufacture. We check the fulfilment of this safety standard using two monitoring processes: a prior one, in which the supplier is informed of the obligatory nature and implications of said standard, and another subsequent one, through which we certify that our products really involve no risks to the health of minors.

Can I obtain information on heel heights and boot leg heights and widths?

To obtain this information, the description in the product page includes details on heel heights and boot leg heights and widths. If you wish to obtain information on boot leg widths, we recommend that you contact our Customer Services Department.
